The house rules.

Read the standards →
01
No sash. No script font.

We refuse pink-glitter clichés. We choose the hotel you'd send your sister to instead.

02
One very good dinner.

Not four mediocre ones. The single best photo of every weekend is at a table with food still to come.

03
We pay for our own rooms.

We're an affiliate site, but we never accept comped stays. The rooms in our index are rooms we paid for.

04
Editorial, not algorithmic.

Every weekend on this site has been tested by an editor with their own group, in their own time, on their own dime.
